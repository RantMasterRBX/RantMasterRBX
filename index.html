About the project

Why Archivarium exists

Web pages disappear quietly. Links rot, servers get switched off, and whole communities of design and writing vanish without a trace. Archivarium is a small effort to keep copies of pages worth remembering, before they're gone for good.

  • Snapshots are captured on a rolling schedule and stored as static copies.
  • Anyone can suggest a page or site for preservation.
  • Everything here is browsable offline — no dead links, no ads, no trackers.
